Who are you?

OPCED Ltd. is an EdTech company. We provide multimedia content for the students through a multi-featured app which is named after the schools. We are upgrading our primitive education system to the modern technology-based education system by providing attractive visual content & AR based content, 24/7 exam, doubts feed and many more. We have content on every topic (English, Math, Science) of NCTB.
We are also making a connection among all the students in our country. The students can connect each other through our 100% verified Edu-social media. Not only students but also teachers, parents and visitors can use the app. All the services are provided to students through the schools.
